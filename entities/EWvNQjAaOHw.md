# How I Use LLMs

In this practical guide, Andrej Karpathy demonstrates how he personally uses large language models in his daily work and life. He covers the ChatGPT interface, different model tiers (free vs plus vs pro), the importance of conversation management (starting new chats when switching topics), file and image uploads for analysis, multimodal capabilities, custom GPTs for repetitive tasks, the memory feature, and practical examples across language learning, coding, research, and content creation. Karpathy also discusses reasoning models, internet search integration, and the broader LLM ecosystem including Claude, Gemini, Grok, and DeepSeek.

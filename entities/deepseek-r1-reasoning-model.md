# DeepSeek R1

## Summary
DeepSeek R1 is a reasoning-focused LLM from Chinese AI lab DeepSeek, introduced January 2025 in the paper "Incentivizing Reasoning Capabilities in LLMs via Reinforcement Learning." It pioneered public revelation of RL-based thinking model methodology — showing emergent behaviors like "wait, let me reevaluate," and response length growing with accuracy on AIME math benchmarks. Its release sparked the "thinking model" race.

## Key Facts
- Released: January 2025
- Developer: DeepSeek
- Key innovation: RL-based reasoning training (vs. pure SFT)
- Notable: response length correlates with accuracy; emergent behaviors
- Hosted on: Together.ai

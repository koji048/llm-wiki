# DeepSeek R1

## Summary
DeepSeek R1 is a reasoning-focused LLM developed by Chinese AI lab DeepSeek, introduced in the paper "Incentivizing Reasoning Capabilities in LLMs via Reinforcement Learning." It pioneered the public revelation of RL-based thinking model methodology, showing emergent behaviors like "wait, let me reevaluate" and response length growing with accuracy on AIME math benchmarks. Its release sparked the "thinking model" race and is widely hosted on Together.ai.

## Key Facts
- Released: January 2025
- Developer: DeepSeek
- Key innovation: RL-based reasoning training (vs. pure SFT)
- Notable: response length correlates with accuracy; emergent behaviors
- Hosted on: Together.ai (US-hosted endpoint)
